My Story

My taste for interior design started thanks to my mother because she was always very interested in modern house designs, this work is completely dedicated to her out of love for her.

My Vision

Interior design is a vital part of daily life, since most of the time we are in our home and if we have a good interior design it gives us certain benefits such as:

Peace
Harmony
Confort

My mision

Research and analyse the main trends in interior design that promote the efficient use of space in modern housing.

Design and build a scale model reflecting an interior layout that optimises the use of space and meets contemporary needs for comfort and functionality./p>

Incorporate sustainable elements in interior design that reduce the environmental impact and improve the energy efficiency of the home.

To realise a modern house with an interior design focused on space optimisation, functionality and comfort, applying sustainable principles and current trends.
